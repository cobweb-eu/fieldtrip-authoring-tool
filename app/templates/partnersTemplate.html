Partners

The Energy Archipelago is a global and self-governed community energy portal that mediates inter-community networking and knowledge exchange, provides insight to the scope and nature of the community energy sector, inspires citizen engagement in the renewable energy transition and places community-led renewable energy in the public spotlight.

The project was originally conceived in 2011 by Scene Consulting, at which time the virtual community energy map was called ‘Scene Connect’. After having mapped out community energy projects throughout the UK, Scene sought partners overseas to expand the initiative globally, with the hope of enabling knowledge exchange between community energy practitioners across the world. The Energy Archipelago is self-funded by its partnership.

We would love to hear from you if you are interested in becoming a partner and contributing to community energy data sharing and knowledge exchange. Please get in touch at info@sceneconsulting.com
